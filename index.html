What We Offer

Z-Ed is an online platform that provides on demand tutoring in a hassle free environment. We also take care of your academic standing by assessing your knowledge through regular exams and drawing up personalized analysis and study plans. You've got all the help you need after school right at your fingertips. Ask your school for your login credentials to start today!

Who We Are

The founders of Z-Ed, current students at SRM University AP and visiting scholars at University of California Berkeley, though of this concept while in the Silicon Valley. Having faced a lot of issues during their secondary education, they hope to make it better for the coming generations. They strive to create a huge network of teachers and students that is easily accessible to those who wish to learn.
